Description

Wisteria is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae (Leguminosae).

The flowers are purple, violet, pink or white.

Wisteria are native to China, Korea, Japan and the USA.

They are popular ornamental plants.

Wisteria have been very commonly used in Japan for family crests as well as many pieces of art.
